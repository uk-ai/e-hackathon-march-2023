Location

Hackathon

The Hackathon will take place in the Recital Room of the Churchill College at the University of Cambridge.

Churchill College address: Storey's Way, Cambridge CB3 0DS

Accommodation

We cover accommodation for participants for Thursday 30th of March 2023. Participants are either allocated at Churchill College or the Møller Institute (See map ).

Churchill College Accommodation

Rooms are available from 14:00hrs on the arrival day and we request that rooms are vacated by 10:00hrs on the morning of departure. The Porters’ Lodge is manned 24 hours a day, so you can arrive at any time of the afternoon (after 14:00hrs) or evening. If checking in after 11:00pm please advise the Porters’ Lodge so that they are aware. T: 01223 336000 E:porters@chu.cam.ac.uk

Facilities in the bedrooms include tea and coffee making facilities, basic toiletries and free Wi-Fi. We have free, on-site parking available on a first-come, first-served basis. As part of our commitment to sustainability, the College has charging points for electric cars which guests are welcome to use.

Breakfast is included and available in the Dining Hall from 07:30 – 09:30am.
